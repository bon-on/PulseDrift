## English

- App Name: Pulse Drift
- Subtitle: A three-lane dodger built for quick reruns
- Short Description: Drag across the lanes, dodge pulse gates, grab sparks, and push your best score higher.
- Promotional Text: Stay early on the lane change, chain clean dodges, and keep the run alive.
- Keywords: arcade, dodger, lane, drag, offline, casual, single-player
- Full Description:

Pulse Drift is a fast mobile dodger built around three-lane movement, clean lane snaps, and escalating pressure.

Swipe or tap across the screen to lock into the nearest lane, slip through incoming pulse gates, and collect sparks for bonus points. The longer you survive, the faster the flow becomes. Your best score is saved automatically so every short run has a clear target to beat.

Features:
- Fast drag-and-tap lane controls
- Endless speed ramp with readable three-lane gameplay
- Spark pickups for score bursts
- Saved best score across launches
- Offline single-player play
